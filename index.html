About Me

I'm Jay Hughes, a web designer and developer based in Houston, Texas. I work with small businesses, ministries, restaurants, and entrepreneurs who want a website that feels like it cost ten times what it did.

My approach is simple: hand-coded, single-purpose sites that load fast, look sharp on every phone, and don't lock you into a monthly platform. You own everything — the code, the design, the domain.

When your business grows, your site grows with it — custom domains, lead forms, analytics, whatever the next step needs.

  • ◆Specialties: brand-first design, custom SVG artwork, interactive data tools
